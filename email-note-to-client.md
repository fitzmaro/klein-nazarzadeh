Quick update on the email setup. You mentioned wanting Outlook with your firm domain — so emails like klein@kninjurylaw.com, nazarzadeh@kninjurylaw.com, etc.

To use a custom domain with Outlook (or Gmail for that matter), there's a monthly subscription involved. Here are the two main options:

**Microsoft 365 Business Basic — $6/user/month**
This is the standard setup for law firms. You get Outlook (desktop + mobile + web), plus Word, Excel, OneDrive, and Teams. Since you're already familiar with Outlook, this is what I'd recommend. For two users that's $12/month.

**Google Workspace — $7/user/month**
Same idea but through Gmail instead of Outlook. Slightly more per user and you'd be using Gmail's interface rather than Outlook.

Both options are legitimate, professional-grade email. There's no free way to get @kninjurylaw.com email — that applies across the board, not just these two.

Since you already bought your domain through GoDaddy, the easiest path is purchasing Microsoft 365 directly through your GoDaddy account. That way the technical DNS records get configured automatically and I won't need to set them up manually.
