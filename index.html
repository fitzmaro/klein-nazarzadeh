Our Story

We Left Big Law
for One Reason

“Injured people deserve more than being a file number at a factory firm. They deserve attorneys who answer the phone.”

At the city's largest personal injury firms, we watched talented attorneys juggle hundreds of cases at once. Clients became file numbers. Phone calls went unreturned. Settlements were rushed to clear the docket.

We started Klein & Nazarzadeh because injured people deserve better. More attention. More preparation. More fight.

When you call our firm, you talk to your attorneys directly. Not an intake coordinator. Not a paralegal screening your call. Us.

We know how the big firms operate because we came from them. We use that experience to build stronger cases and fight harder for every client who walks through our door.

Injury Cases

Car crashes, truck collisions, rideshare incidents, motorcycle accidents, and pedestrian injuries throughout New York City and the surrounding boroughs.
